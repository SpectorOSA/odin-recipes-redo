Pastrami Sandwich

Source: Ryan Liebe

This recipe by Alejandro Valdes Lora will have you making your very own delicious pastrami sandwich in no time!

Ingrediants

  • 4 tbsp. unsalted butter, softened
  • 4 tbsp. yellow mustard, plus more for serving
  • 8 slices. rye bread
  • 1 lb (454g). cooked sliced pastrami

Instructions

  1. Step 1

    Butter one side of each slice of rye bread. Turn half of slices buttered side down. Spread unbuttered sides with mustard. Spread pastrami over mustard. Close sandwiches buttered side up.

  2. Step 2

    Heat a medium skillet over medium heat. Toast sandwiches, turning halfway through, until fragrant and warmed through, about 3 minutes per side.

  3. Step 3

    Transfer sandwiches to a platter. Serve with more mustard alongside.
